HabitCore Privacy Policy

HabitCore is a planning and consistency app. This policy explains what stays on your device, what advertising services process, and the choices available to you.

Data kept on your device

Plans, Goals, Tasks, notes, schedules, reminders, progress, focus sessions, history, achievements, and preferences are stored locally. HabitCore does not upload this content to a HabitCore account or attach it to advertising requests.

Local data remains until you delete it, clear app storage, restore a different backup, or uninstall HabitCore. Because there is no HabitCore account storing this content, the developer cannot retrieve or delete it remotely.

Advertising and privacy choices

HabitCore uses Google AdMob. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect or share IP address or general location, ad and app interactions, diagnostics, and device or advertising identifiers for ad delivery, measurement, analytics, security, and fraud prevention. Google encrypts this data in transit and may process it in other countries.

Ads may be personalized, non-personalized, or limited according to your region and choices. Google's User Messaging Platform requests consent or offers opt-out controls where required. You can revisit available choices in Settings > Privacy > Ad privacy choices and manage the Android Advertising ID in device settings.

HabitCore does not sell your Plans, Goals, Tasks, notes, history, or backup content to advertisers.

Imports, exports, sharing, and Ask AI

These actions occur only when you choose them. Review files and prompt previews before confirming. Content sent to messaging, storage, or AI apps is then handled under that provider's terms and privacy policy. HabitCore does not automatically send Ask AI content or import an AI response without your confirmation.

You are responsible for the content you enter, import, export, share, or act on, including choosing recipients and protecting backup files. HabitCore is a productivity tool and does not guarantee third-party or AI-generated content. This does not limit rights that cannot legally be limited.

Your controls and security

  • Edit, export, or delete local app content in HabitCore.
  • Withdraw or change available advertising privacy choices.
  • Reset or delete the Android Advertising ID in device settings.

Local content relies on your device's security. No storage or transfer method is completely secure, so protect your device and exported files.

Children

HabitCore is not designed to knowingly collect children's personal information on HabitCore-operated servers. Advertising treatment is configured according to the app's published audience settings and applicable requirements.

Third parties, changes, and contact

Learn more in the Google Privacy Policy and Google's partner data explanation. This policy may change when HabitCore's features, providers, or legal obligations change. For privacy questions, use the developer contact email shown on HabitCore's Google Play listing.

By using HabitCore, you acknowledge this policy. Where consent is legally required, HabitCore asks separately through the applicable privacy message; acknowledgement is not a substitute for consent.

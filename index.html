HabitCore Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

HabitCore is a habit, task, routine, and consistency tracking app. This policy explains how the app handles your data.
Local-first by design

Local storage

HabitCore stores your habits, recurring tasks, routines, progress, and generated insights locally on your device.

No personal data collection

HabitCore does not collect, upload, sell, or share your personal information with external servers.

No advertising

HabitCore does not use advertising SDKs and does not sell user data.

Export and import

HabitCore may allow exporting and importing habit or task files. Any files you choose to share are controlled entirely by you.

Future updates

If future versions introduce cloud synchronization, analytics, accounts, crash reporting, or online services, this privacy policy may be updated accordingly.
